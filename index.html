Welcome to Team Chimera

About Us

Started in the year 2006, Team Chimera is the first group in India to successfully transcend into the automotive sector of hybrid and electric technology. We are a multi-dimensional team comprising of students with back grounds in various engineering disciplines such as Electrical and Electronics, Mechanical, and Computer Science, apart from a dedicated team to look after the management and business development aspects of the team. Team Chimera has, till date, undertaken various ventures, which include: E-volve Triple Powered Vehicle(TPV), Hybrid Auto Rickshaw, a plugin hybrid from the Electric Vehicle - REVA and the latest being building a Formula Hybrid race car and Formula Electric race car from scratch.

With the cost of gasoline skyrocketing to new heights and the predictions concerning its depletion within the next 20 years, electric and hybrid automotive technology provides a logical solution.

Our participation in recent events such as Formula Hybrid Competition, USA, Formula Electric Competition, Italy and Student Formula, Japan, Formula Green and Formula Bharat have been immensely successful and we continue to strive for progress and expansion in these environment friendly sectors.

Reva Electric Plug-In Hybrid.

We have worked in conjunction with REVA Electric Car Company, National Instruments and Bosch India to conceptualize a fully automated Series Plug in Hybrid Vehicle. This concept, in simple terms, uses an I.C Engine to charge the batteries, both of which are present on board the vehicle. The batteries are in turn, used to power a DC Traction Motor which propels the vehicle. With such a system one realizes all the benefits of an electric vehicle overcoming the only drawback, range. This is done by the onboard engine which charges the batteries. This brings the range of the vehicle close to 500km.

FSAE Hybrid

First Asian team to participate.

We have developed a formula hybrid vehicle for the 2014 FSAE competition held in the month of April-May in New Hampshire USA.

Our team was placed 9th all over the competition among 35 universities from all over the world. The Hybrid vehicle is a high performance open-wheeled race car built under a set of constraints and parameters to ensure participant safety, while allowing for student creativity.

The Formula Hybrid Competition attracted significant attention from media and people from all over the world. Taking the Fan Favourite Award back home, Team Chimera marked a milestone to its excellence and reputation.

Unveiling of Electric car

It is the first electric car of RV College of Engineering and one of the few electric cars from India contesting in ATA Italy 2015.

The occasion was graced by the Principal of RVCE, HODs and professors from various departments and our fellow students. We received a lot of appreciation as well as valuable feedback from them all, which we promise to consider. We'd like to thank our Sponsers, RVCE and the RSST, our fellow students and everyone else who helped bring "Vidyut Pragethana" to reality.

FSAE Electric

First Electric car of RV College of Engineering

Throughout modern times we have witnessed many advancements in technology. These advancements are results of research and data collected about our world and society, helping us to better understand the most efficient ways we can work in. With this increased knowledge, we have become more aware of the footprint humans leave on the environment.

One major advancement we have seen in our environment conscious world is the Electric Car. The Electric car as we know makes use of electric motor and batteries for propulsion. The motor provides the car instant torque , which gives smooth and strong acceleration. With decreased emissions, minimal gas usage, and increased safety, the electric car is surely the car for the future . We have seen the Tesla Roadster and the Mahindra Reva which are the stepping stones towards the world of Electric cars.

We at Team Chimera are trying to contribute towards this by building a Car powered by electricity. This will be our first venture into pure Electric cars after spending years in making Hybrid cars which make use of gas along with electricity for propulsion. The event which we are striving for is the ATA Formula Electric, jointly organised by SAE, which will take place at Varano de Melegari circuit in Italy during the month of September, 2015.

FSAE Japan-2016

E-VOLVE v2

This year, the team built its second electric race car E-VOLVE V2 and participated in Student Formula Japan. The team grabbed 6th position globally in ELECTRIC VEHICLE category and got THE ROOKIE AWARD for being THE BEST TEAM among all the participating teams.

E-VOLVE V2 had a powerful three-phase induction motor of 65HP(peak), 111Nm torque and 7500rpm. It had a CURTIS motor controller, Battery pack of 96v rating and comprises of thirty cells, each of 3.3v and 60Ah rating. Car weights approximately 220kgs. The car can reach a maximum speed of 140kmph and can go up to 40km with a single charge.

This car had CAN connectivity and had a seven inch LCD display. This gives it an edge over other similar cars.

Formula Bharat 2018

Formula Bharat is an engineering design competition in which students from colleges and universities all over the country, compete with a life-size Formula-style vehicle in areas of engineering design, overall cost, marketability and dynamic performance. The series replicates the global student Formula series hosted in around 11 countries per year.

Understanding that electric vehicles are poised to take over the future of mobility, Formula Bharat this year introduced the Formula Student Electric Vehicle Concept Challenge, which had 19 participating teams along with the 55 others competing in the Formula Bharat combustion category.


Team Chimera visited Kari Motor Speedway, Coimbatore from January 25 th to 28 th 2018 to participate in the event. As it was a Concept Challenge it had statics category and the statics events include Business Plan Presentation(BPP),Cost Report, Design Report.

The team secured the following positions:


Sixth in Business Plan Presentation

Fifth in Cost Report

Tenth in Design Report


This was a great moment for the team as it was our first time participating in this event. After giving our best efforts we woke up the next morning to Eighth place overall.

Formula Green 2018

CHRONOS

February 12th, 2018, Team Chimera heads off to Kari Motor Speedway to compete with this seasons electric car, CHRONOS 2.0 in Formula Green.

Formula Green is a Design Challenge initiated by Indian Society of New Era Engineers to enhance the student's approach in engineering and diploma pursuing students. The main intention of this competition is to design and fabricate a formula vehicle which runs on electric power bound to some specific rules and regulations.

Arriving to Coimbatore on 12th the team started prepping the car for technical inspection and the static events. After being given a tech number, we had to prepare the car as well as prepare for the static events. The team was able to get into technical inspection minutes before they closed for the day where we found there were some minor things to be fixed before we could compete in the dynamic events.

From tech, it was on to Cost Report, the Business Plan Presentation(BPP) followed by Design Report.

We secured the following positions:


Third in Cost Report

Third in Business Plan Presentation

Third in Design Report

Over all we finished third in the statics.

After a successful tech inspection, we moved on to the dynamic event i.e. acceleration, skid pad, autocross and endurance. Our performance in the dynamics made us stand out from other participating teams.

Third place at Formula Green was a great way to finish off and we realized that there was plenty to improve in the prototype for upcoming events.

We would like to thank our sponsors and friends for their continuous help and support for this event! Without them, we would not be able to celebrate successful events like these. We look forward to some more exciting competitions.

Formula Bharat 2019

CHRONOS

Team Chimera participated in Formula Bharat 2019 held in Coimbatore, India. It is an engineering design competition in which students from all over the country, compete with a life-size Formula-style vehicle in areas of engineering design, overall cost, marketability and dynamic performance.

Our journey started off by having pre technical inspection on the first day. Both the E Wing and the Mech Wing worked really hard to get through the inspection round. With the aim of winning the competition, we came back with same enthusiasm and spirit on the second day. The preliminary round of Business plan presentation was held. The judges were very happy with our plan and they also recommend some improvements. Meanwhile, the technical inspection was also held where our designs were highly appreciated and were compared to industrial standards.

The design event tested the engineering skills of the team. It was followed by Cost Report presentation. On the same day, the results of Business plan presentation were announced and we were into finals along with 5 others teams. The BP team made the necessary edits needed and were ready for the finals. The finals of BPP was stage event where we had to present our plan in front of a Audience and were judged by panel of 10 judges. The amount of support we received from the audience was overwhelming.

Fourth day started off with great excitement as the results were displayed. The judges were immensely happy with our design but there were some technical errors in the accumulater and safety of the car was a big concern. Unfortunately, we weren't able to clear the technical inspection. We are very proud and happy to announce that Team Chimera won the Business Plan event. This was the first time we won the BPP and this boosted the teams confidence to a great level.

The complete results are as follows


Eighth in Cost Report

First in Business Plan Presentation

Fifth in Design Report

Formula Bharat gave clear insights of our car and its performance. This event helped us understand our teams strengths and at the same time we realised our flaws. Overall, we return back with great experience and we wish to improve and give a better performance in our upcoming event.

FSAE Japan-2017

CHRONOS

The team set out for Ogasayama Nature and Sports Park ECOPA, JAPAN roughly 6000 km away to participate in Student Formula Japan 2017.

We were one of the 94 teams which participated in the event. This was also our first international event in 2017. CHRONOS had a powerful three-phase induction motor along with Curtis motor controller. With a top speed of 140 kmph and an acceleration of 0-75m in 5.5 seconds our model was one of the best amongst others competing. It had a charging time of 75 minutes and an endurance of 40 km.

To win a competition, things have to go perfect, and that didnt occur for us this time, due to issues with our logistics which got delayed due to bad weather conditions. And hence we werent able to participate in the dynamic events, although our team cleared preliminary technical inspections.

The team stood 6th in Business Plan Presentation among in the electric category.

In the end the team was happy and satisfied with their performance and desired to strive harder and come back stronger next year.

We would like to thank our sponsors and friends for their continuous help and support for this event! We are grateful to them as they have stood by us during our highs as well as our lows. We would not have been able to reach higher goals without their support.
